Quadcopter (Most Common)

  • Hover perfectly in air

Fixed-Wing (Airplane Style)

  • Long flights (1+ hour)
  • Great for mapping fields
  • Needs runway to takeoff
  • Nepal farming favorite

Hybrid Drones

  • Best of both worlds
  • Future of Nepal delivery
  • Most expensive type
